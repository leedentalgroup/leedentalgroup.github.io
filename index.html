COVID-19 Safety

With the on-going COVID-19 global pandemic, our clinic has been working hard to follow the guidelines provided by the ADA, CDC, and WHO.

The health and safety of our staff and patients are our top priorities as we get ready to reopen in June 2020.

We thank you all in advance for your cooperation, understanding, and patience as we maneuver through this pandemic together.

With that in mind, here are some guidelines and changes we have implemented at our clinic:
